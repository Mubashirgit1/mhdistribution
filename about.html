Our Journey & Milestones

Since our founding, we have been on an incredible journey of growth and success. In February 2024, M&H was established with a vision to provide quality products and exceptional service in the e-commerce space. By July 2024, our business had gained momentum, expanding across multiple platforms like Amazon, eBay, and TikTok. Our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction fueled rapid growth, allowing us to reach a broader audience and build a strong online presence. Now, in March 2025, we have proudly achieved a major milestone—hitting £90K in sales. This achievement is a testament to our hard work, strategic expansion, and loyal customers who trust our brand. As we continue on this path, we remain committed to providing top-quality products, competitive pricing, and an outstanding shopping experience for our customers and business partners.
